How To Play

Win the lane in ten rounds

1. Build defense

Tap a tower card, then tap one of your five slots during prep. Stronger towers cost more mana.

2. Send pressure

Queue attackers into the next wave to score when they cross the lane before the AI stops them. Each scoring creep also grants 1 bonus mana for your next round.

3. Keep pressure up

Rounds continue automatically with bonus mana. You also gain 1 bonus mana next round for every enemy creep you destroy, so offense and defense both fuel your economy.
