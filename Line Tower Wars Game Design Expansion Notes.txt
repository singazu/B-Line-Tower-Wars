Line Tower Wars Game Design Notepad

PvP
- Can certain creeps attack other creeps?
- Should we have a wave cap? Yes
- Should points scored gain mana?
- Should killing creeps gain mana?
- Pathing? Or stick to fixed positions? Or a small batch of fixed positions?


Rankings and rewards... why should people play consistently?
- Earn in-game-currency (which needs a great name) to buy any of the real currency options. Literally ANY of them. If you play long enough, you can get the $2499 platinum skins. 
- Ratings. Should we go flat % or use a numerical system like Chess, where you don't exactly have to say you're 45% if you're playing against mostly greats?


Cosmetics
- team-generated selection to include high-ticket-price options like GOLD $999 and PLATINUM $2499 specifically for rich people. Also nods to non-protected games like Chess, where towers and creeps look like classic chess pieces. 
- artist-generated selection for specific styles like Jon Carling or PenUSBmic at more reasonable prices, but with rev-share for said artists, giving them reason to cross-promote our game. 
- we could even do licensed skins like My Little Pony or TMNT or South Park, which could be HUGE and cheap.


Marketing
- Focus heavily on ads of challenging your friends to duels. Who gets shotgun when we go out tonight? Duel. Who gets the best room in our Airbnb? Duel. ***How can we become Kleenex of tissues???
- Absolutely NO pay-to-win. Like Chess, everyone has the same starting point. 
- Twitch streamers challenging other Twitch streamers. Literally nobody flies solo. It's all PvP. Play to their egos.
